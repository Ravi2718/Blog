The Journey to Becoming an Ethical Hacker

Everyone’s journey into ethical hacking is different. Some start with curiosity, others with a desire to protect and serve. But what unites them is passion for security.

My Starting Point

I began by learning how websites work, then I got curious about how they can break. That curiosity turned into researching vulnerabilities, trying CTFs, and diving into cybersecurity blogs.

Major Turning Points

  • Discovering TryHackMe and Hack The Box
  • Breaking into my first web app lab
  • Achieving my first CEH certification

Challenges I Faced

From information overload to imposter syndrome, the path wasn't easy. But each failure taught me persistence.

Advice to Beginners

  • Stay consistent — 1 hour a day is enough to grow steadily
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for help on forums and communities
  • Practice > Theory
Stay focused, stay hacking.
— Ravi
